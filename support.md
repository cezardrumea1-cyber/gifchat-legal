## Common Questions

### How do I delete my account?

Go to **Settings → Delete Account**. This permanently removes your account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

### How do I report inappropriate content or a user?

Use the **report option** available on posts and profiles within the app. Long-press a post or tap the menu icon on a profile to access the report feature.

### How do I reset my password?

Go to the login screen and tap **"Forgot password?"**. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox to set a new password.
