## Important: Use of Artificial Intelligence

**GifChat is built around AI. To turn your text into reaction GIFs, the content you type — including the text of your messages and conversation context — is sent to third-party AI providers (OpenAI and Anthropic) for processing.** This is essential to how the app works. Specifically:

- **OpenAI** receives your message text, post captions, and search queries to generate "embeddings" used to match your words to relevant GIFs.
- **Anthropic (Claude)** receives your message text and surrounding conversation context to break messages into emotional segments so the app can pick fitting GIFs.

These providers process this content to provide the GIF-matching service. We do not use this content to build advertising profiles. Please review OpenAI's and Anthropic's privacy policies to understand their data practices. **By using GifChat's messaging and GIF features, you consent to this AI processing of your content.**

If you are not comfortable with your message content being processed by these AI providers, please do not use the app's messaging or GIF-generation features.

## Information We Collect

### Information you provide

- **Account information:** your email address, password (stored securely/hashed by our authentication provider), and username.
- **Profile information (optional):** display name, bio, and profile picture (avatar).
- **Content you create:** posts (GIFs, captions, titles, hashtags), messages (text, GIF attachments, voice recordings, replies, forwards), stories, and comments.
- **Age confirmation:** confirmation that you meet the minimum age requirement at signup.

### Information collected automatically

- **Usage and engagement data:** likes, post views, story views, reposts, saves, and similar interactions. Some view tracking is recorded per-viewer (i.e., which user viewed which post/story).
- **Social graph:** your follows, follow requests, and interaction streaks.
- **Inferred preferences:** we automatically generate a "taste profile" (a vector representation of your GIF preferences based on your interactions) and language-affinity scores to improve GIF matching and content relevance.
- **Activity status:** a "last seen" timestamp updated when you use the app.
- **For advertisers only:** if you use advertiser features, we log IP address and user-agent for ad impression/click events.

### Information we do NOT collect

We do not collect your location, health data, calendar, contacts, or browsing activity outside the app. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, cookies, or device fingerprinting.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and operate the app (create your account, deliver messages, show your feed, generate GIFs).
- Match your text to relevant GIFs using AI (see "Use of Artificial Intelligence" above).
- Moderate content for safety (see "Content Moderation" below).
- Personalize your experience (relevant GIFs and content based on your taste profile and language affinity).
- Send you push notifications (e.g., when someone interacts with you).

## Content Moderation

To keep GifChat safe, images and videos you upload are scanned by **AWS Rekognition** (a third-party service) before they are published, to detect explicit, violent, or otherwise prohibited content. Image and video frames are sent to AWS for this purpose.

## How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the third-party service providers necessary to operate the app:

| Provider | What is shared | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **OpenAI** | Message text, post captions, search queries | AI GIF matching (embeddings) |
| **Anthropic (Claude)** | Message text + conversation context | AI message segmentation for GIF selection |
| **AWS Rekognition** | Image/video frames you upload | Content moderation |
| **Supabase** | Account and app data | Database, authentication, and file storage |
| **Railway** | App data in transit | Backend hosting |
| **Expo (push notifications)** | Push tokens, notification content | Delivering push notifications |
| **Stripe** | Payment details (advertisers only) | Processing advertiser payments |

Each provider processes data under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a business transfer.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Deleting your account and data

You can delete your account at any time from within the app (Settings → Delete Account). When you delete your account:

- We permanently delete your personal and identifying data, including your profile, email, account, posts, private messages, stories, saved items, and uploaded media (including your profile picture).
- Content you contributed to shared spaces (such as comments on other users' posts or messages in group chats) is **anonymized** — your identity is removed (shown as "[deleted]") so that other users' experience is not broken, but the content itself may remain.
- Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

### Other rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. **If you are in the European Union/EEA, you have rights under the GDPR.** To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **gifchatlegal@gmail.com**.
